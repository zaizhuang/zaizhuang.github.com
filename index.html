Zaizhuang Cheng

Full stack web engineer. Startup junkie.

Hi there, my name is Zaizhuang and I am a full stack web engineer. I started my first website in 2005 on the LAMP stack. Since then, I've been the 1st engineer at a startup, led a team of 14 developers on an enterprise web application, hacked on a few side projects for fun and done my part to nuture Singapore's budding startup ecosystem.

 

Background

I accepted a government scholarship to study in USA and enrolled in Purdue's School of Electrical & Computer Engineering in Fall 2003. In Spring 2006, I graduated top of my class earning myself a Bachelor of Science in Electrical Engineering (Highest Distinction).

I moved on to Stanford in Fall 2006 to do a Masters of Science in Management Science & Engineering, which I finished in Spring 2007.

It was this year at Stanford that got me addicted to the world of entrepreneurship and set me on the path to become a web engineer. I left USA with a heavy heart in 2007 for Singapore to fulfil my 6 year government bond.

 

Current

I'm currently at Defence Science & Technology Agence (DSTA) in Singapore. I lead a team of 14 developers in the design and implemention of an enterprise web application based in ASP .NET, which will be used by 4,000 internal staff.

In the past year, I trained my team in agile development methodologies like pair programming and test driven development, and delivered the enterprise application on time and within specifications.
